About OpenHazards

Pioneering earthquake forecasting to protect lives and property since 2009

Our Mission

To help people worldwide protect themselves and their homes from severe natural disasters by supplying free, reliable information about earthquake risks and potential property damage.

OpenHazards Group specializes in earthquake forecasting and natural hazard analysis. We bring together experts in earth science, physics, and technology to provide tools and information aimed at reducing the impact of natural disasters.

Since our founding in 2009, we've been committed to making advanced hazard assessment tools available to everyone—from homeowners to government agencies—not just professionals.

Our Journey

2009

Company Founded

OpenHazards Group established in Davis, California. Launched the world's first free, open, global earthquake hazard viewer.

2013

Real Estate Services

Introduced Natural Hazard Disclosure reports for California property transactions, providing faster and more affordable alternatives.

2015

Mobile Launch

Released QuakeWorks iOS app, bringing earthquake forecasting to mobile devices with location-based alerts.

2020

Enterprise Focus

Pivoted to B2B licensing of PI/NTW engine for insurance and catastrophe bond analytics.

2025

Platform Relaunch

Major website rebuild and modernization to better serve both public users and enterprise clients.
